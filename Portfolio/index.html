Privileged access management

Privileged accounts (or administrative or highly empowered accounts) are magnets for cyberattackers, this particular PAM is based on windows server 2010 A PAM project will highlight the controls needed to protect these accounts, which should be prioritized according to level of risk. PAM projects should cover human and non-human system accounts and support a combination of on-premises, cloud, and hybrid environments, as well as APIs for automation. The features of the PAM consist of Active Directory, group Policy, DHCP & Routing (NAT), DNS & IIS and Hyper-V. Although it must be said, the whole project is still under critical working, and not everything surpasses the penetration and encounters critical vulnerability.
